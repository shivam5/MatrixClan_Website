ABOUT US

The Matrix Clan is the official computer club of Mount St. Mary's School, Delhi Cantt. The club consists of members from various classes who unite to contribute immensely to the technology environment in the school. All the members actively take part in interschool competitions and organize various school events including the Matrix Ecomm Symposium.
