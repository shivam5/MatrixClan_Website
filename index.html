About Us

The Matrix Clan is the official computer club of Mount St. Mary's School, Delhi Cantt. Striving to make a mark in the field of technology and computer science, the club consists of enthusiastic members - each of them contributing in their unique way.

Members

The club consists of members from various classes who unite to contribute immensely to the technology environment in the school. All the members actively take part in interschool competitions and organize various school events.
